What if there were a clearer, more profound way to think about climate change?

Imagine you've filled a pot with water and ice. (Lots of ice.)

You put your hand inside. How cold is it?

You grab your handy thermometer to find out.

nerd tidbit

0° Celsius is the equilibrium at which water and ice coexist.

The temperature will read 0° Celsius

(Or 32° Farenheit if you're an old fart.)
